Intel’s Sustainability Timeline

1968

Robert Noyce and Gordon Moore rename NM Electronics to Intel Corporation, laying the foundation for decades of innovation.

1980s

Intel expands operations while adopting early sustainability practices.

1990s

Intel launches recycling and water conservation programs worldwide.

2000s

Intel increases renewable energy use and invests in green design.

2010

Intel achieves new efficiency benchmarks in chip manufacturing.

2015

Intel expands corporate social responsibility and clean energy goals.

2018

Intel becomes a recognized leader in sustainability rankings.

2020

Intel commits to 100% renewable electricity use globally.

2022–Present

Intel pledges net-zero greenhouse gas emissions by 2040.
